About us

Who We Are

Welcome to Hadir

At Hadir, we’re transforming the way Egyptians interact with government services. Our mission is to eliminate the barriers of traditional bureaucracy by providing a user-friendly, secure, and centralized platform for all your essential needs. Whether you’re applying for an ID, registering a vehicle, or tracking the status of your applications, Hadir empowers you to complete tasks effortlessly, anytime, anywhere. We believe in making government services accessible and efficient for everyone. By integrating cutting-edge technology and collaborating with government entities, we’re building a digital future for Egypt—one service at a time.
